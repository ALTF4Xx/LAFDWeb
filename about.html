About LA:RP Los Angeles Fire Department

LAFD was founded in early 2024, with one goal in mind, to ensure safety of civilians and others alike. We have ensured this goal through determination, professionalism and exemplary conduct..

The LAFD has grown into one of the largest and most respected departments in LARP, staffed by highly trained and dedicated firefighters and medics.
