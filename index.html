About the Los Angeles Fire Department

The Los Angeles Fire Department (LAFD) is committed to protecting lives, property, and the environment through fire prevention, emergency medical services, and disaster response.

LAFD was founded in early 2023, with one goal in mind, to ensure safety of civilians and others alike. We have ensured this goal through determination, professionalism and exemplary conduct. The LAFD has grown into one of the largest and most respected departments in LARP, staffed by highly trained and dedicated firefighters and medics.
