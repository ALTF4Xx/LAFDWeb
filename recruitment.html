Requirements for LARP: LAFD FireFighter

  • Must be at least 13 years old.
  • Answer all questions fully.
  • Have some knowledge about how Fire Departments work.
  • Have some knowledge of Firefighting, and firefigthing procedures.
  • Strong commitment to public safety and community.

Requirements for LARP: LAFD Medic

  • Must be at least 13 years old.
  • Answer all questions fully.
  • Have some knowledge about how Fire Departments work.
  • Have some knowledge of medical treatments.
  • Strong commitment to public safety and community.
